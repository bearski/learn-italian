## After Day 14
- **Phase 2 (Weeks 3–4):** Translation Ladder Mon–Sat + free write Sunday.
- **Month 2 onward:** 5-Sentence Journal every morning — think in Italian, no dictionary.
- **Every Sunday:** 30-minute review of your Mistakes notebook.

## The rules that matter most
1. **Every word gets a sentence** — never copy a word in isolation.
2. **Write by hand, say it aloud** — pen-to-paper is the whole point.
3. **The Mistakes notebook is sacred** — never let an error sit uncorrected.
4. **Describe around** words you don't know instead of reaching for a dictionary.
