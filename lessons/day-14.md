# Day 14 — Free Write
**Week 2 · Focus: Produce**

Write **5–8 sentences** about your week, your plans, or a place in Italy you want to visit.

Rules:
- Use **only words you know**. No dictionary.
- If you don't know a word, **describe around it** — this is what fluency actually feels like.
- Mix tenses if you can: *Ieri ho... / Oggi voglio... / Domani devo...*

🎉 You've finished the two-week foundation. **Next:** Phase 2 (Translation Ladder Mon–Sat + free write Sunday), then from Month 2 the **5-Sentence Journal** every morning. Every Sunday: 30-min Mistakes review.
