# 📚 Free Online Resources — ranked for *your* goal

Near-zero → A1/A2, listening-heavy (the questura appointment is ~80% listening). At this level **daily listening reps beat any single textbook**. Put ~70% of your time into audio, and use the written resources only to look things up.

---

## ⭐ Start here — FSI Italian (free, public domain, PDF + audio)

The U.S. Foreign Service course — built for adults who need to *function* fast through drilling and listening. The closest thing to a free "boot camp" textbook **with sound**.

- **All FSI Italian courses (hub):** https://www.livelingua.com/project/fsi/italian
- **FSI Programmed Italian** — best first step for a near-zero adult: sound system + basic grammar through guided drills.
  https://www.livelingua.com/course/fsi/italian_programmed_course
- **FSI Italian FAST — Volume 1** — situational, function-fast dialogues; closest to your questura/court skill.
  https://www.livelingua.com/course/fsi/italian_fast_course_-_volume_1

**How to use:** start with **FAST Vol. 1** for situational listening; dip into **Programmed** when you need a grammar point explained.

## 🎧 Audio courses (your #1 priority)

- **Language Transfer — "Complete Italian"** — completely free, audio-only, brilliant for building intuition on the move. Best single free resource for learning to *think* in Italian.
  https://www.languagetransfer.org/
- **Coffee Break Italian** — free podcast seasons, gentle A1→A2 progression.
  https://coffeebreaklanguages.com/coffeebreakitalian/

---

## 📖 Written textbooks & courses (free)

- **Open University — Beginners' / Portfolio Italian** — modern, CEFR-aligned mini-courses (food, travel, introductions) with audio.
  https://www.open.edu/openlearn/ *(search "Italian")*
- **Wikibooks: Italian** — a complete free structured course; good written backbone alongside the audio.
  https://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Italian

---

## 🔎 Grammar & verb reference (free, look-up only)

- **Grandgent, "An Italian Grammar"** — public domain, complete; matches the rules in your [Foundations](00-foundations.md). Read online / download.
  https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/search/?query=Italian+grammar+Grandgent
- **Conjuguemos — Italian verbs** — free conjugation drills.
  https://conjuguemos.com/
- **Wiktionary conjugation tables** — type any verb, e.g. *capire*.
  https://en.wiktionary.org/
