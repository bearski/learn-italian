# Day 12 — Recall Phrases
**Week 2 · Focus: Cover & Recall**

Cover Day 11. Write **all 14 phrases from memory**. Circle every mistake.

Then role-play: write a 4-line fake exchange using at least 3 phrases.
*— Mi scusi, quanto costa? — Dieci euro. — Va bene, grazie mille! — Prego.*
