# Day 13 — Translation Ladder
**Week 2 · Focus: Build a sentence brick by brick**

Start your **Translation Ladder**. Take one base sentence and grow it across the steps:

| Step | Add | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Subject + verb | Io mangio. |
| 2 | a place | Io mangio a casa. |
| 3 | a time word | Io mangio a casa stasera. |
| 4 | an adjective | Io mangio una pizza grande a casa stasera. |
| 5 | make it negative | Io non mangio a casa stasera. |
| 6 | make it a question | Mangi a casa stasera? |

Do the full ladder with *Io mangio*, then try a second base verb of your choice.
