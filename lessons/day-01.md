# Day 01 — Function Words
**Week 1 · Focus: Copy & Say**

Write **all function words**, saying each aloud. For every word, write a short sentence on the same line.

| Italian | English | Example | Translation |
|---|---|---|---|
| e / ed | and | pane e acqua | bread and water |
| ma | but | voglio ma non posso | I want to but I can't |
| o | or | caffè o tè? | coffee or tea? |
| di | of / from | una tazza di caffè | a cup of coffee |
| a | to / at | vado a Roma | I'm going to Rome |
| in | in | vivo in Italia | I live in Italy |
| con | with | vengo con te | I'll come with you |
| per | for | è per te | it's for you |
| su | on / about | un libro su Roma | a book about Rome |
| da | from / since | vengo da Milano | I come from Milan |
| che | that / which / who | la persona che conosco | the person (that) I know |
| sì | yes | Sì, grazie! | Yes, thanks! |
| si | oneself / one | si chiama Marco | his name is Marco |
| no / non | no / not | non capisco | I don't understand |
| anche | also / too | anch'io! | me too! |
| più | more | voglio più caffè | I want more coffee |
| molto | very / a lot | molto bene! | very well! |
| solo | only / just | solo un momento | just a moment |
| già | already | ho già mangiato | I've already eaten |
| ancora | still / again | ancora una volta | once again |
| sempre | always | arrivi sempre tardi | you always arrive late |
| mai | never | non vado mai lì | I never go there |
| qui / qua | here | vieni qui! | come here! |
| lì / là | there | è là | it's there |
| come | how / like | come stai? | how are you? |
| quando | when | quando arrivi? | when are you arriving? |
| dove | where | dove sei? | where are you? |
| perché | why / because | perché sei triste? | why are you sad? |
| se | if | se vuoi, vieni | if you want, come |

**Daily routine (15 min):**
1. **Copy & Say (5 min)** — write each word by hand, say it aloud, and add a short sentence on the same line. Never copy a word in isolation. *voglio → Voglio un caffè.*
2. **Cover & Recall (5 min)** — cover the Italian, write it from memory. Every miss goes in your **Mistakes** notebook, circled.
3. **One Sentence (5 min)** — take 2–3 words and make one sentence. Simple wins. *Voglio un cane grande.*

> 🔎 **Foundations hook:** before you start, read **0.1 Pronunciation** — get *c/g*, *gli*, *gn*, *sc* and double consonants right from word one (*perché*, *già*, *anche*).
