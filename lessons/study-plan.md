# 🗓️ Study Plan — 3 × 20 min, weekdays, 8 weeks to questura-ready

**Your budget:** three 20-minute sessions a day, Mon–Fri = **5 hours/week, ~40 hours over 8 weeks.** That is genuinely enough to walk into the questura prepared and to hold a tennis match — *if* the three daily sessions do three different jobs.

> **The one idea that makes this work:** don't study the same way three times a day. Each slot targets a different brain mode and a different skill. New input when you're fresh, listening when you're not, output when you can speak aloud. The spacing between them *is* the spaced repetition.

---

## The daily template (every weekday)

| Slot | When (suggested) | Job | What you do |
|---|---|---|---|
| ☀️ **S1 — INPUT** | morning, fresh | Learn something new | Today's lesson / grammar / mission section. Read it, write it by hand, say it aloud. |
| 🌤️ **S2 — LISTEN** | lunch / commute | Train the ear | Audio only (Language Transfer / FSI FAST). React *physically* or repeat aloud. No reading. |
| 🌙 **S3 — DRILL + PRODUCE** | evening | Lock it in & speak | Anki deck (10 min) **+** write 3 original sentences and say them out loud (10 min). |

**Always:** 📖 Dictionary · ✍️ Daily Writing · ❌ Mistakes notebooks beside you. Every word gets a sentence; every miss goes in Mistakes.

**Weekends:** rest. Optional 10-min Anki on Saturday to hold the streak — never required. Sunday off completely.

**Busy-day fallback (the "minimum viable day"):** if two sessions die, the one that survives is **S3 Anki**. Ten minutes of review beats a skipped day to zero.

## The 8-week arc

| Week | ☀️ S1 INPUT (morning) | 🌤️ S2 LISTEN (midday) | 🌙 S3 DRILL+PRODUCE (evening) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | Mon [Foundations](00-foundations.md) · Tue–Fri [Day 01–04](day-01.md) | Language Transfer #1–10 | `core-vocab`: function words → top verbs · write 3 sentences |
| **2** | [Day 05–09](day-05.md) | LT #11–20 + first FSI FAST dialogue | `core-vocab`: verbs + nouns · 3 sentences |
| **3** | [Day 10–14](day-10.md) | FSI FAST Vol. 1, early lessons | `core-vocab`: all tags · Day 14 free-write |
| **4** | [Questura](questura-permesso.md) §1–3: phrases YOU say | **Officer commands §4 as audio** — mime each action | `missions`: `questura-say` + `questura-personal` |
| **5** | Questura §5–6: personal info cold + numbers/dates | Officer commands until automatic | Role-play both sides aloud, **record yourself** · `questura-listen` |
| **6** | Mon–Wed run the full §6/§7 scene · Thu–Fri start [Tennis](tennis.md) scoring | FSI FAST + officer-command mix | Full questura scene ×3 · `tennis-score` + `tennis-court` |
| **7** | **Book 2–3 italki lessons** — "role-play a questura appointment" | Tennis match script (§6) by ear | Real italki = your **Stakes** · tennis small talk aloud |
| **8** | Light review only — no new material | Re-listen to your weakest audio | **Daily full questura dry-run** · all decks on review |

> Week 8 is a **taper/buffer** — schedule the real appointment for the end of Week 8 or later. You walk in over-rehearsed, not cramming.

## How the pieces map to slots

- **S1 INPUT** → the markdown lessons (Foundations → Day 01–14 → mission files).
- **S2 LISTEN** → [resources.md](resources.md): Language Transfer first, then FSI FAST audio. Weeks 4–5 swap in your recorded officer commands.
- **S3 DRILL** → the two Anki decks ([core-vocab](anki/core-vocab.csv), [missions](anki/italian-missions.csv)). Turn on Italian TTS so IT→EN cards train listening too.

---

## Rules that keep it honest

1. **Three different jobs, three sessions** — never just re-read. Input ≠ listen ≠ produce.
2. **Speak aloud in S1 and S3** — silent study doesn't build a mouth that can answer an officer.
3. **The Mistakes notebook is reviewed every Saturday** (the optional 10 min).
4. **Protect S3** — if the day collapses, Anki is the non-negotiable core.
5. **Stakes are real** — the Week 7 italki booking is the appointment that makes the other 39 hours count.
