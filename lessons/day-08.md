# Day 08 — People & Places
**Week 2 · Focus: Nouns**

Write the **People & Life** and **Places** nouns. Say each aloud; add a sentence per noun.

**People & Life:** uomo/donna (man/woman), bambino/bambina (child m/f), famiglia (family),
amico/amica (friend m/f), persona (person), gente (people), vita (life), giorno (day),
anno (year), tempo (time/weather), mondo (world)

**Places:** casa (home/house), città (city), paese (town/country), via/strada (street/road),
posto (place/spot), ufficio (office), negozio (shop), scuola (school), ristorante (restaurant)

> 🔎 **Foundations hook:** every noun has a gender. -o usually masculine, -a usually feminine, -e either.
> Pair each with its article from **0.2**: *il giorno, la casa, l'amico, lo studente*.
